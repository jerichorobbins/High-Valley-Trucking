About Us

Founded by Michael Willoughby, High Valley Trucking is now operated by three generations of drivers. It is a family-run business that takes pride in safe, reliable propane and utility transport across the Mountain West.

“We’re not just in the trucking business — we’re in the legacy business. Every load we haul is a reflection of the generations before us.”
— Gibeon Robbins, Owner & Operator

Meet the Team

Michael Willoughby – Founder & Owner
With decades of experience, Michael started High Valley Trucking to build a legacy. He still drives and mentors the younger generations.
Rodney Robbins – Veteran Driver
Rodney has been behind the wheel for nearly three decades. His experience and attention to detail keep every load on track and on time.
Gibeon Robbins – Owner & Lead Operator
As a fourth-generation driver, Gibeon combines tradition with modern trucking standards, including hazmat-certified propane and utility delivery.
